Welcome to Gavel Conference 

Initiated by the Gavel Club of University of Moratuwa, the Gavel Conference is an event focused on uniting all the Gaveliers and non-Gaveliers of universities, private institutions and schools island-wide. It is a great chance and a privilege for all participants to witness an event that will undoubtedly be life-changing. The experience they gain by participating in the sessions will be one-of-a-kind and it will add great value to their professional careers. The conference aims at guiding budding professionals to become effective speakers and leaders and serve this nation in the near future with their utmost potential.

Main Events

All Island Best Gavel Speaker 2016

The contest is held with the motives of uncovering hidden talents of budding young speakers in Sri Lanka and encouraging them to polish their skills and to strive towards success. The participants of the conference will get the opportunity to witness the Grand Finale during the day of the conference. The contest will be conducted in full compliance with Toastmasters International standards, with the participation of Toastmasters as judges.

Model Gavel Meeting

A demonstration of a methodical gavel meeting will be conducted, along with explanations of each role player and position holder within a Gavel club, with the motive of enhancing the quality of meetings conducted by university undergraduates, especially of Gavel clubs recently established. The General Evaluator of the meeting would be an experienced Toastmaster.
